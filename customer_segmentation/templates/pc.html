Potential Customer

These are customers who have the specific characteristics like 50 lakhs annual income and with a minimum age requiremnt of 35 and above. They also possess under-graduate of education. these customers have the medium potential to be a long term customer but are thrifty spenders.They are also more likely to join the loyalty programme of the company which are economical.


Guidelines

  • Set up a reward points system
  • Send them 2nd tier offers unavailable for other customers
  • Have a subscription auto-reorders option if your products allow it
  • Engage them in the product creation and actively ask for feedback
  • Relate number of orders to discount tiers

Choose any one of the loyalty programmes to enroll the customer
